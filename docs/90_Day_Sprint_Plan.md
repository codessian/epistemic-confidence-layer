## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### **If Grants Are Slow:**
- Increase consulting offerings (ECL integration support)
- Create paid courses or workshops
- Offer "office hours" or mentorship for $150-300/session
- Partner with AI agencies as technical advisor

### **If Technical Challenges Arise:**
- Reach out to AI research community for help
- Break down scope into smaller milestones
- Focus on one dimension of confidence at a time
- Document challenges publicly (builds trust)

### **If Burnout Threatens:**
- Take 2-3 day break (guilt-free)
- Delegate tasks to AI partners more aggressively
- Reduce social media engagement temporarily
