# ECL Foundation (Planned)

## Vision
The Epistemic Confidence Layer will be stewarded by an independent, non-profit foundation to ensure:
- **Neutrality**: No single vendor controls the protocol
- **Transparency**: Open governance and decision-making
- **Sustainability**: Long-term maintenance and development
- **Community**: Inclusive participation from all stakeholders

## Proposed Structure

### Board Composition (7 seats)
- **Research** (2 seats): Academic institutions, AI safety labs
- **Industry** (2 seats): Major AI companies, enterprise users  
- **Community** (1 seat): Open source contributors, developers
- **Ethics** (1 seat): AI ethics, responsible AI organizations
- **Legal** (1 seat): Legal expertise, compliance, standards

### Governance Principles
- **Open Standards**: Protocol specifications remain open and royalty-free
- **Reference Implementation**: Maintain canonical open-source implementation
- **Certification**: Optional compliance testing and certification program
- **Research**: Fund research into calibration, uncertainty, and trust metrics

## Timeline
- **v0.4**: Form foundation working group
