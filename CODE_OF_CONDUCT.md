# Code of Conduct

We are building public-interest infrastructure. Conduct yourself with respect and integrity.

## Pledge
We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully.

## Enforcement
The maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
Report issues privately as described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to project maintainers, who will take appropriate action.
